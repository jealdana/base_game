INTRODUCTION

Household energy use is an important contributor to many environmental problems including air and water pollution and global warming. Many are seeking ways to encourage communities to use less energy.

In this experiment, you will play a household energy conservation challenge. It is a real financial investment game where you will make decisions with 5 other Amazon Mechanical Turk workers. As residents of the apartment building you are trying to reduce your collective energy usage by 30%. You will earn a smaller or larger financial incentive, depending on your and others’ energy choices.

Next, you will get instructions for the game, a trial game and a quiz (an opportunity to earn extra). Then, you’ll proceed to the actual game. To make this experiment a success, please read the following rules of the experiment very attentively.
