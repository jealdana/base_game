THE GAME STRUCTURE

There are 6 players who represent people living in the same apartment building. Thus, in addition to you, there are 5 other players. The building is trying to meet a shared energy conservation goal of reducing the annual energy use by 30%* through resident actions. Hence, the House Conservation Fund will need to achieve 216 (equal to $2.16) by the end of the game.

Every player faces the same decision problem. At the beginning of the experiment, you will receive 120 energy tokens ($1.20) in your private account. During the experiment, you can invest your tokens either for personal benefit or use them to contribute to the House Conservation Fund. In the end:

  • Token in your private account will be paid out to you in cash.
  • If your group reaches the goal, the amount contributed to the House Conservation Fund will be doubled and divided evenly between the 6 participants.
  • Each $10 contributed to the energy conservation account will equate to an actual removal of 1 Metric Tons of air pollution (by donating to Carbonfund.org).

You will be making your decisions anonymously.
