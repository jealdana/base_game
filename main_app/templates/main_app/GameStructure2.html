THE GAME PLAY

During the experiment: You will play 12 rounds (1 round for each month of a year). In each month, you can choose to invest some of your energy tokens to the House Conservation Fund that aims to reduce your building’s energy consumption and reduce air pollution and the associated health risks.

In each month all 6 players are asked simultaneously: “How much do you want to invest into House Conservation Fund?” You may contribute up to 10 energy tokens ($.01) each month. Only when all players have made their choices will the month’s results be displayed to all players. After each month, the computer will credit each player’s contribution to the House Conservation Fund and deduct them from their private accounts.

At the end of the game (after 12 months) the computer will compare the HHouse Conservation Fund balance against the predetermined goal of 216 tokens ($2.16). Meeting the goal requires every player to contribute on average 3 tokens ($.03) each month into the House Conservation Fund. If the ConservationFund has 216 tokens or more, the amount invested into the collective pot will be doubled and divided back to participants evenly. The amount in the House Conservation Fund will also be matched with a donation to Carbonfund.org, where each $10 will equate to an actual removal of 1 Metric Tons of air pollution.
